## about

[![Ask DeepWiki](https://deepwiki.com/badge.svg)](https://deepwiki.com/khrnchn/sedekah-je)

sedekah.je is a directory of qr codes of mosques, suraus, or other islamic institutions (eg: tahfiz) in malaysia.
the primary goal is to make it easier for people to donate to these institutions.

## contributing qr

use the [contribution form](https://sedekah.je/contribute). click on "log masuk" to login and you will be redirected to the contribution page. you can also monitor your submissions at [my contributions](https://sedekah.je/my-contributions) page and there is also a [leaderboard](https://sedekah.je/leaderboard).

## contributing code

everyone is welcomed to contribute! fork the repo and start cooking. you may also propose an idea by creating an issue.
