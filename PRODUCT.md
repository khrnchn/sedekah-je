# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Malaysians who want a quick, trusted way to find verified DuitNow QR codes for mosques, suraus, and Islamic institutions. The main public context is mobile-first discovery: search, filter by category or state, inspect a QR code, and donate through familiar Malaysian payment rails. Contributors and maintainers also use the product to submit, review, and keep QR data accurate.

## Product Purpose

sedekah.je exists to make legitimate donation QR codes easier to discover and share. Success means a person can find the right institution with low friction, feel confident the QR is maintained by a transparent open-source community, and complete their donation without the interface getting in the way.

## Brand Personality

Calm, transparent, useful. The product should feel community-maintained and trustworthy, not over-marketed. Copy should be direct and minimal because the working directory and QR utility matter more than persuasion.

## Anti-references

Avoid corporate Islamic banking polish, generic SaaS dashboard styling, festive poster-heavy Ramadhan visuals as the default system, noisy gradients, and decorative copywriting. The interface should not look like a campaign microsite or a fintech sales page.

## Design Principles

1. Trust before decoration: verification, legibility, and predictable controls matter more than visual novelty.
2. Let the directory work: search, filters, QR previews, and institution cards should stay fast to scan.
3. Respect local familiarity: Malaysian states, DuitNow, mosque and surau categories, and Bahasa Malaysia labels should feel native.
4. Keep the open-source tone: simple structure, visible utility, and modest styling should signal transparency.
5. Polish the system, not the pitch: improve tokens, spacing, hierarchy, and states without turning the product into a marketing page.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Accessibility is not the primary project priority, but the public donation flow should maintain practical baseline quality: readable contrast, clear focus states, touch-friendly controls, reduced unnecessary motion, and QR previews that are not visually compromised by the surrounding theme.
